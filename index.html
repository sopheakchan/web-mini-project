6 STEPS to add your test

01.

Get your MCQs ready

Prepare yourself a proper file or get the link of the site of MCQs that is reachable by us.

02.

Send us through FB page

Send to our Facebook page (QuizBox) the file or the link to access the MCQs.

03.

Tell us about it

Briefly explain the test and its purpose. We'll ask a few questions to determine if it should be posted on our website.

04.

Acceptance

If the MCQ test benefits a large number of students beyond a small private group, we will consider accepting it

05.

Test being created

After acceptance, we will create the test. The time needed depends on the type and number of MCQs and our current workload.
